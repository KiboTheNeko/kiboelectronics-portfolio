About

I'm a hardware engineer and embedded systems enthusiast focused on designing intelligent robotic systems and optimization techniques for resource-constrained devices.

With expertise in robotics control, embedded software, and hardware design, I've worked on projects ranging from autonomous quadruped robots to IoT trainer kits and microcontroller-based applications.

My passion lies in bridging the gap between theoretical research and practical implementation, creating systems that are both innovative and accessible.
